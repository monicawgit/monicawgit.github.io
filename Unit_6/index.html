What is User Centered Design?

Human Factors Engineers design automation to “meet users where they are” in order to avoid catastrophic human errors. Usability was a principle tenet. As the internet became the main way to do business, usability became critical to keeping users from clicking away, and user centered design became a focus for businesses.
